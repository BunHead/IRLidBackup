Proof of Personhood

Proof of personhood is a method to verify that a digital identity belongs to a unique human, not a bot or duplicate account.

Why It's Important

Distinguishing real users from automated entities helps defend against fraud and enables fair participation online.

The IRLid Solution

IRLid enables two people to meet and mutually confirm co-presence via QR scans, producing a signed receipt without requiring a backend.
